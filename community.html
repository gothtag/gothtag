Who We Are

GOTHTAG is a goth community born on Discord and active on platforms like TikTok and Instagram. We bring together artists, writers, musicians, creators, and people who simply enjoy goth culture, music, and aesthetics.

The community is open, chill, and welcoming from baby-bats to longtime goths, alt people, and the curious. We also support goth creators, brands, and influencers by helping them connect and gain visibility, without pressure or gatekeeping.
