Frequently asked questions about GOTHTAG and our community.

What is GOTHTAG?

GOTHTAG is a digital collective of artists, creators, and visionaries who explore themes of darkness, mystery, and the occult through various forms of creative expression. We are bound by silence and united in our appreciation for gothic aesthetics and shadow work.

Is GOTHTAG a cult?

No, we are not a cult. We are a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for gothic and occult themes. Our "cult" references are aesthetic and thematic, not literal. We embrace the dramatic flair of occult imagery while maintaining a respectful, inclusive space for all members.

How do I join GOTHTAG?

To join our community, you must submit an application through our website. We review each application carefully to ensure cultural fit and shared values. Membership is selective but accessible to those who genuinely resonate with our aesthetic and philosophy.

What kind of content do you create?

Our community creates diverse content including digital art, photography, poetry, music, writing, web design, and other creative expressions that explore gothic, occult, and mysterious themes. We value authenticity over trend-following.

Do you have age restrictions?

Yes, all members must be 18 years or older. Our content often explores mature themes and we maintain a space appropriate for adults who can engage with complex, sometimes dark, subject matter.

Is GOTHTAG active on social media?

Yes, we maintain a presence on Discord, Instagram, TikTok, and Linktr.ee. However, our primary focus is on creating meaningful content rather than constant social media engagement. We believe in quality over quantity.

What does "bound by silence" mean?

"Bound by silence" refers to our philosophy that true understanding comes from creation and contemplation rather than endless discussion. We communicate through our art and respect each other's creative process without unnecessary chatter.

Do you collaborate with other creators?

Yes, collaboration is encouraged within our community. We believe that combining different creative visions can lead to powerful, unique works. Many of our most compelling projects emerge from collaborative efforts between members.

How often do you post new content?

Content creation varies by individual. Some members are highly prolific, others create more deliberately. We value quality and meaningful work over frequent posting. Our community provides space for all creative rhythms.

Can I submit my existing work?

Absolutely. We encourage members to share their portfolios and existing work. Our community is a space for mutual inspiration and appreciation of each other's creative journeys.

What if my application is rejected?

If your application doesn't align with our current community needs, you may reapply in the future. We periodically review our membership criteria and welcome applications from those who continue to evolve their creative practice.

Still Have Questions?

Our community values direct experience over endless questions. Sometimes the best way to understand is to join and see for yourself.
